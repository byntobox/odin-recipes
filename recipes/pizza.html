Pizza


A delicous pizza good for any meal or event!

Pizza is a popular Italian dish consisting of a round, flat base of dough, typically topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings such as meats, vegetables, and herbs. It is baked in an oven until the crust is crisp and the cheese is melted, resulting in a savory and versatile meal that can be customized to suit different tastes.

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough:
    • Flour
    • Water
    • Yeast
    • Salt
    • Sugar (optinal)
    • Olive Oil
  • Pizza Sauce
    • Tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes
    • Garlic
    • Olive oil
    • Salt
    • Oregano
    • Basil
    • Black pepper
  • Cheese:
    • Mozzarella (traditional choice)
    • Parmesan (optional, for extra flavor)
    • Other cheese options (optional): cheddar, provolone, ricotta
  • Toppings (optional, based on preference):
    • Pepperoni
    • Sausage
    • Ham
    • Bacon
    • Mushrooms
    • Bell peppers
    • Onions
    • Olives
    • Fresh basil or arugula
    • Tomatoes
    • Pineapple
    • Anchovies

Cooking Instrustions:

  1. Prepare the Dough:
    • In a large bowl, mix together flour, yeast, salt, and sugar (if using).
    • Slowly add water and olive oil while stirring until the dough comes together.
    • Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes until smooth and elastic.
    • Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour, or until it doubles in size.
  2. Prepare the Sauce:
    • In a small bowl, mix together tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes with garlic, olive oil, salt, oregano, basil, and black pepper.
    • Taste and adjust seasoning as needed.
  3. Assemble the Pizza:
    • Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
    • Once the dough has risen, punch it down and roll it out on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
    • Transfer the rolled-out dough to a pizza stone or a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
    • Spread the prepared pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
    • Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese (and any other cheeses you like) over the sauce.
    • Add your favorite toppings such as pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, etc.
  4. Bake the Pizza:
    • Place the pizza in the preheated oven and bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  5. Serve:
    • Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a couple of minutes.
    • Slice and serve hot.
