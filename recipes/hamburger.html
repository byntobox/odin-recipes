Hamburger


A juicy burger great with some fries and an ice tea!

A hamburger is a classic dish featuring a seasoned beef patty, grilled and served in a toasted bun. It's typically topped with lettuce, tomato, onions, pickles, and cheese, with condiments like ketchup or mustard. Versatile and satisfying, the hamburger is a favorite comfort food enjoyed by many.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Burger buns
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Cheese slices (optional)
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato slices
  • Onion slices
  • Pickles
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Mayonnaise
  • Bacon (optional)

Cooking Instrustions:

  1. Prepare the Beef Patties:
    • In a bowl, season the ground beef with salt and black pepper.
    • Divide the beef into equal portions and shape them into round patties, slightly larger than the burger buns.
  2. Cook the Patties:
    • Preheat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat.
    • Place the patties on the grill or skillet and cook for 3-5 minutes on each side, or until they reach your desired level of doneness.
    • If using cheese, place a slice on each patty during the last minute of cooking to melt.
  3. Toast the Buns:
    • While the patties are cooking, lightly toast the burger buns on the grill or in a toaster until golden brown.
  4. Assemble the Burgers:
    • Place the cooked patty on the bottom half of each bun.
    • Add lettuce, tomato slices, onion slices, and pickles on top of the patty.
    • Spread ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise on the top half of the bun, or use your preferred condiments.
    • If desired, add bacon or other toppings.
  5. Serve:
    • Place the top bun on the assembled burger.
    • Serve hot and enjoy your homemade burger.
